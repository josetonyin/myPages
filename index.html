1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us
In Short: We collect information that you choose to create or store in the app.
Athmana lets you read Bible passages and create personal notes, reflections, highlights, drawings, and exported note documents. If you create content in the app, that content is stored on your device.
Sensitive Information. We do not intentionally process sensitive personal information.
Application Data. If you use our application, we may process the following information if you choose to provide access or use the related features:
  • Geolocation Information. Athmana may request access to your location when creating a note so the note can optionally include where it was created. Location use is optional and can be disabled in your device settings.
  • Mobile Storage and Files. Athmana stores notes and app data locally on your device and may create exported files such as note documents or PDFs when you use sharing features.
  • iCloud Documents. If iCloud is available and enabled on your device, notes may sync through your iCloud account across your Apple devices.
  • Diagnostics. We may receive standard Apple crash and diagnostic information made available through Apple's platform services.
All information you choose to store in Athmana should be accurate and appropriate for your own use. You can change app permissions in your device settings.

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?
In Short: We process your information to provide the app's reading, note-taking, syncing, exporting, and support features.
We may process your information for the following purposes:
  • To display Bible verses and language-specific content inside the app
  • To save your notes, highlights, drawings, and verse references
  • To attach optional location details to notes you create
  • To sync notes across devices using Apple's iCloud services when available
  • To generate shared note files or PDFs when you use export or share features
  • To diagnose crashes, improve app performance, and maintain security

3. WHAT LEGAL BASES DO WE RELY ON TO PROCESS YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?
In Short: We process personal information only when we have a valid legal basis, such as your consent, our legitimate interests in operating the app, or compliance with law.
Depending on your location, these legal bases may include:
  • Consent. For example, when you grant location access or choose to use app features that store your content.
  • Legitimate Interests. To operate, secure, and improve Athmana.
  • Legal Obligations. When disclosure or retention is required by applicable law.
  • Vital Interests. In rare cases where processing is necessary to protect someone from harm.

4. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?
In Short: We share information only in limited situations necessary to operate the app or comply with law.
We may share your personal information in the following situations:
  • Apple Services. If you use iCloud syncing, your note data may be stored and transmitted through Apple's iCloud infrastructure subject to Apple's terms and privacy practices.
  • Sharing Actions You Initiate. If you export or share notes or PDFs, the selected content is shared using the iOS share sheet and the destination you choose.
  • Legal Requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers. We may share or transfer information in connection with a merger, sale, financing, or acquisition.

5. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?
In Short: We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this notice unless a longer period is required by law.
Notes and related app content are generally kept until you delete them, remove the app, or remove them from your iCloud storage. Some copies may temporarily remain in device backups or iCloud according to Apple's systems and retention practices.

6. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?
In Short: We aim to protect your personal information through reasonable technical and organizational safeguards.
No electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should use the app and share exported content only in environments you consider secure.

7. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?
In Short: Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information and to withdraw consent.
You may also manage much of your information directly in the app or on your device by deleting notes, removing shared files, disabling location permissions, or turning off iCloud syncing.

Because Athmana is a mobile app and not a web tracking service, it does not currently respond to browser Do-Not-Track signals.
