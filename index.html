About The App

Athmana is a Bible reading, reflection, and prayer app designed around a clean, distraction-light experience. Version 1.4 adds a full guided prayer section with bead-by-bead timed playback for the Rosary and Divine Mercy Chaplet, a Live Activity widget on the lock screen and Dynamic Island, and three-language support (English, Malayalam, Spanish). The Bible reading experience includes quick book search, verse range mode, and a reflections workspace for saving verses and writing notes.

  • Three-language interface: English, Malayalam, and Spanish
  • Fast book search with filtered results and abbreviation support
  • Single verse or verse-range reading with chapter and verse controls
  • Save-to-Notes flow for collecting verses into named reflections
  • Notes area with text and drawing modes plus export/share tools
  • Guided prayer section with Rosary, Divine Mercy Chaplet, Way of the Cross, and custom prayers
  • Bead-by-bead timed prayer playback with adjustable pace
  • Live Activity showing current prayer step on lock screen and Dynamic Island
  • Background audio keep-alive so prayer timer runs when the screen is off
  • iPhone and iPad layouts

Feature Highlights

Three-Language Reading

The app supports English, Malayalam, and Spanish, with localized labels, placeholders, prayers, and reading output in each language.

Book Search And Selection

Users can type a full book name or abbreviation, browse filtered matches, and jump directly into a selected book.

Verse Range Mode

The interface supports chapter selection plus from/to verse controls, making it easier to read or save verse spans instead of only single verses.

Save To Notes

Verses can be saved into named reflections, and the screenshots show confirmation feedback after a verse is saved.

Reflections Workspace

Saved notes include verse cards, editable thought sections, text formatting tools, drawing support, and management actions.

Spanish Support

Version 1.4 adds Spanish as a third language, with localized prayers and a two-language picker system alongside Malayalam and English.

Timed Prayer Playback

Bead-by-bead guided playback for the Rosary and Divine Mercy Chaplet, with adjustable timer settings and a visual Rosary bead map.

Live Activity Widget

A Prayer Widget extension shows the current prayer step on the lock screen, in the Dynamic Island, and on CarPlay — updating in real time as each bead advances.

Background Prayer Keep-Alive

Silent background audio and a background refresh task keep the prayer timer running even when the screen is off or the app is not in focus.

Cross-Device Experience

The UI adapts across phone and tablet layouts while preserving the same reading, notes, and prayer workflow.

iPad Layout

Passage And Save Flow

Selected verses can be reviewed and saved into notes from the main reading screen.

Search Suggestions

Typeahead book search supports abbreviations and displays matching books while you type.

Saved To Notes

Athmana confirms when a verse has been saved into a reflection, keeping the workflow lightweight.

Reflections List

Notes are organized as reflections with verse counts and creation dates.

Reflection Editor

Saved verses, writing tools, formatting controls, and note-editing features are available inside each reflection.

Home And Language Picker

The home screen surfaces Bible reading, Prayers, and Notes. A language picker lets you choose a second language — Malayalam or Español — alongside English.

Timed Rosary — Prayer Step

The timed prayer view shows the current prayer text, step counter, and a visual Rosary bead map. Playback controls let you pause, rewind, or skip beads.

Timed Rosary — Bead Progress

As each bead advances, the map highlights the current position and the mystery subtitle keeps track of where you are in the decade.

Create Custom Prayer

Build your own prayers with a title, icon, and any number of body sections — organized alongside the built-in Rosary, Divine Mercy, and Way of the Cross prayers.
