About The App

Athmana is a Bible reading, reflection, and prayer app designed around a clean, distraction-light experience. Version 1.12 introduces a dedicated Read tab — a full chapter-by-chapter Bible reading experience with a Continue Reading progress tracker, Old and New Testament browsing, numbered verse display, verse bookmarks, inline highlighting, Save to Note, Open on BibleGateway, and Search in Google — all from a single tap on any verse. First launch downloads Bible translations (ICB and Malayalam) during a guided setup flow. The app also supports three languages (English, Malayalam, Spanish), seasonal prayer awareness, timed bead-by-bead prayer playback with Live Activity on the lock screen and Dynamic Island, and Prayer Connect community rooms.

  • Dedicated Read tab with chapter-by-chapter Bible reading across all 66 books
  • Continue Reading card that tracks your current book, chapter, and percentage progress
  • Old Testament and New Testament toggle for fast book browsing
  • Per-verse context menu: Bookmark, Highlight, Save to Note, Open on BibleGateway, Search in Google
  • Bible Version selection (ICB) and Bible Downloads with guided first-run setup
  • Three-language interface: English, Malayalam, and Spanish
  • Scripture Search tab for single verse or verse-range lookup with chapter and verse controls
  • Save-to-Notes flow for collecting verses into named reflections
  • Notes area with text and drawing modes plus export/share tools
  • Prayer Connect — create or join shared prayer rooms with family and friends via a room code
  • Group Prayer Board showing each member's prayer count and room total in real time
  • Prayer requests tab with categories, urgency levels, and anonymous posting
  • Room reflections tab for sharing thoughts and scripture within your prayer group
  • Seasonal prayer awareness with Current Season banner and liturgically relevant prayer suggestions
  • Guided prayer section with Rosary, Divine Mercy Chaplet, Way of the Cross, Novena, St. Michael, Memorare, and custom prayers
  • Bead-by-bead timed prayer playback with adjustable pace
  • Live Activity showing current prayer step on lock screen and Dynamic Island
  • Background audio keep-alive so prayer timer runs when the screen is off
  • iPhone and iPad layouts

Feature Highlights

Read Tab — Chapter Reading

A dedicated Read tab lets you browse all 66 books by Old or New Testament, tap into any book, and read full chapters with numbered verses in a clean, focused layout.

Continue Reading Tracker

The Read tab remembers your place and shows a Continue Reading card with your current book, chapter, and percentage progress through the book.

Verse Actions

Tap any verse to get a context menu: Bookmark the verse, Highlight it, Save to Note, Open on BibleGateway, or Search in Google — all in one step.

Bible Version And Downloads

Choose your Bible translation (ICB included). On first launch a guided setup screen downloads the selected translations before entering the app.

Three-Language Interface

The app supports English, Malayalam, and Spanish, with localized labels, placeholders, prayers, and reading output in each language.

Scripture Search

The Bible tab lets you search by book name, set chapter and verse controls, toggle Verse Range mode, and save directly to a reflection — with confirmation feedback on save.

Reflections Workspace

Saved notes include verse cards, editable thought sections, rich text formatting tools (Bold, Italic, Heading, Bullet, Quote, Divider, Checkbox), drawing support, and management actions.

Prayer Connect — Community Rooms

Create or join prayer rooms with a short code and pray together with family and friends in real time. Manage multiple rooms and see your Creator or Member role at a glance.

Group Prayer Board

Each room member's prayer count is tracked and displayed on a shared board, with a running total for the whole group broken down by prayer type.

Prayer Requests And Reflections

Room members can post prayer requests with categories and urgency levels, pledge to pray for others, and share scripture reflections inside the room.

Seasonal Prayer Awareness

A Current Season banner surfaces liturgically relevant prayers like the Resurrection season at the top of the Home screen and Prayers section.

Timed Prayer Playback

Bead-by-bead guided playback for the Rosary, Divine Mercy Chaplet, Way of the Cross, Novena, St. Michael, Memorare, and custom prayers, with adjustable timer settings.

Live Activity Widget

A Prayer Widget extension shows the current prayer step on the lock screen and in the Dynamic Island, updating in real time as each bead advances.

Cross-Device Experience

The UI adapts across iPhone and iPad layouts while preserving the same reading, notes, and prayer workflow on every screen size.

App Screens

Screenshots showing the main Athmana experience across iPhone and iPad, including the new Read tab introduced in version 1.12.

Home Screen

Version 1.12 adds a Read tab to the navigation. The home screen shows the Current Season banner, Today's Prayers quick grid, and shortcuts to Bible Version and Bible Downloads.

Read Scripture — Old Testament

The Read tab opens with a Continue Reading card showing your current book, chapter, and progress percentage, then a full grid of Old Testament books to browse.

Read Scripture — New Testament

Toggle to New Testament and browse all books with their chapter counts. Each book tile opens directly into chapter reading.

Reading View — Verse Actions

Tap any verse while reading to reveal a context menu: Bookmark, Highlight, Save to Note, Open on BibleGateway, or Search in Google.

Scripture Search

The Bible tab lets you pick a language, search by book name, set chapter and verse controls, and save a verse directly to a reflection.

Reflections List

Notes are organized as named reflections with verse counts and creation dates, searchable from the top of the list.

Reflection Detail

Saved verses appear inside each reflection. Below them a rich text editor with Bold, Italic, Heading, Bullet, Quote, and more lets you write personal thoughts in English or Malayalam.

Prayer Connect — My Rooms

The Community tab lists all your prayer rooms with their room codes and member counts. Create a new room or join one with a short code.

Prayer Room — My Prayers

Inside a room the Prayers tab lists all prayers with tap-to-log counters. The Group Prayer Board below tracks each member's total and the room-wide count.
